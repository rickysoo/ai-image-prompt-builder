Be Specific

Use detailed descriptions rather than vague terms.

✓ "Bright crimson Ferrari 458 Italia" ✗ "Red sports car"

Describe the Scene

Include setting, time of day, weather, and atmosphere.

✓ "Rainy evening in Tokyo streets with neon reflections"

Use Art Keywords

Add style descriptors to guide the AI.

"digital art", "oil painting", "photograph", "pencil sketch"

Quality Modifiers

Include quality terms for better results.

"high quality", "detailed", "4K", "ultra-realistic"

Camera Technical Details

For photorealistic results, include camera specifications.

"shot with Canon EOS R5, 85mm lens, f/1.4, shallow depth of field, professional lighting"

Lighting Terminology

Master lighting keywords for dramatic effects.

"Golden hour", "rim lighting", "soft box", "dramatic shadows", "volumetric lighting"

Composition Rules

Apply photography composition principles.

"Rule of thirds", "leading lines", "symmetrical composition", "close-up portrait"

Artist References

Reference specific artists and styles.

"In the style of Van Gogh", "Pixar animation", "Studio Ghibli", "Renaissance painting"

Negative Prompts

Specify what you don't want in the image.

"--no blurry, low quality, distorted, extra limbs, text, watermarks"

Color Theory

Control mood and atmosphere with color.

"Monochromatic blue palette", "warm autumn colors", "high contrast", "pastel tones"
